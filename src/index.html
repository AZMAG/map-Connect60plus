Information on navigating around this map

  • Map - The map area will show a geographical location with corresponding search results and other information
  • Layers - Hover over the widget in the righthand corner of the map to view available layers for your location
  • Info Window - When you click on a marker in the map an info window will appear that displays additional information about that particular place.
  • Street View - Drag the yellow pegman on the map's zoom controls to a location on the map to view and navigate street-level imagery
  • Points of Interest - Points of Interest tab identifies types markers on the map.
  • Address - Search for a location on the map by entering an address in the search box in the Address tab.
  • Directions - Get directions by entering a starting and ending location in the Directions tab, and click Get Directions. Directions appear on the map as a blue line. Waypoints are specified by green (start) and red (end) markers. You can drag any of the markers on the map to change directions on the fly. You can also change the type of directions by changing the travel mode drop down box. Click on suggested routes to see alternitive route directions. Use the Clear Directions button to clear the map and start over.
  • Report a problem - Help to make Google Maps better by reporting data or product issues by clicking on "Report a map error" on the bottom of the map.
For more information on using Google Maps please visit - Google Support
